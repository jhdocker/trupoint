How can I use this?

This application is designed to help you when you are overwhelmed with a challenging interaction.
Go through this tool and inject your own unique situation into each category and let it help guide you to conflict resolution.


What is this for?

This is a tool developed by members of Trupoint to enable workers to deal with stressful situations in their work and home lives. It can be a literal step by step instructions, or a general reminder of how we should approach problems in our lives.
