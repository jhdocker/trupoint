Clarity


  • What is the problem or challenge?

  • what emotion am I feeling?
    • (Anger/Fear/Anxiety/Confusion/Hoplessness)

  • Why am I feeling this emotion?
    • (Blocked expectations / Control issues/ An event / Failure/ Disappointment)

  • What aspects of this issue (cause of my emotional reaction) do I acctually have control over?

  • What aspects of this issue do I want to change but have no control over?

  • What are the decisions that are mine to make in this situation?

  • Identify the ways in which I have caused harm in the situation.

  • What is the next thing to do in this situation?

Truth

  • I am powerless over certain aspects of this situation.

  • There is a power in the universe that may influence the outcome in ways I may not understand or agree with.

Action

  • I will clean up any mess that I have made.
    • Own it, make amends, apologize

  • I will set aside those things I do not have control over and remove their power to influence my emotions.
    • Setting aside yesterday's regrets and tomorrows fears.

  • I will make decisions on those items I have control over to move the issue toward a positive resolution.
    • Toward success and peace

  • I will act on those things I have control over by doing the next right thing.
    • My goal is progress, not perfection
